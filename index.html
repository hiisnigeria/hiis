Frequently Asked Question

We have tried to answer some of the questions we get often below.

What is HIIS?

HIIS is an acronym for Health Insurance Innovation Systems. which is actually a body of health insurance innovative products we are contantly working on.

Why Health Insurance?

There is shortage of innovative products tackling challenges in health insurance. It is a domain constantly troubled by fraud and financial misappropriations. we are tackling that.

Why Blockchain?

Through transparency, immutability, non repudation and permissions on the blockchain, we can tackle most of the issues encountered in Claims Management Processing.

Who will use it?

The HIIS system will be adopted for the management of health insurance claims and enrolees management by National Health Insurance Schemes, Governments, and key players in the health insurance space. This includes HMOS, Hospitals, and other Care Providers.

How can i Contribute?

If you are a domain expert, whether in Health Insurance or Blockchain? and will like to contribute, just reach out to us via email or phone call.

Is Our Solution Software or Hardware?

The HIIS Claims and Enrolees Management System is entirely software run on the blockchain platform.

How Easy is its usage?

We are building a system that is user friendly and easy to use by all parties involved in the health insurance claims management process.

Is there any learning curve for begginers?

All users of our systems will be trained on usage before deployments
